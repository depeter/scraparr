# European Event Sources - Comprehensive Analysis 2025

## Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive country-by-country analysis of the most valuable event scraping sources across Europe. Platforms are ranked by market dominance, event coverage, and scraping value.

**Key Market Consolidation Trends:**
- **PLG (Piletilevi Group)** is rapidly consolidating Central/Eastern Europe (Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Czech Republic, Slovakia, Poland)
- **CTS Eventim** dominates Germany, Austria, Italy, and has significant presence across 20+ countries
- **Ticketmaster/Live Nation** has major presence in UK, Ireland, Nordics, and Western Europe
- Regional platforms often provide better local event coverage than international giants
